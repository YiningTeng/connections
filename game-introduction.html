Game Introduction

Welcome to Endless Connections Game, a fun and challenging word puzzle game!

How to Play

You are given 16 words. Your goal is to group them into 4 sets of 4 words that share a common theme. Each correct group awards 100 points, while each incorrect guess costs 10 points and reduces your lives.

Where the Connections Come From

The words and their connections are generated using the Gemini API, which ensures a diverse and engaging set of themes and categories. The game dynamically adjusts the difficulty based on your score, making it suitable for players of all skill levels.

History

This game is inspired by classic word association puzzles and modernized with AI-generated content to provide an endless variety of challenges.
