Endless Connections Game

Game Introduction

Welcome to Endless Connections Game, a fun and challenging word puzzle game!

How to Play

You are given 16 words. Your goal is to group them into 4 sets of 4 words that share a common theme. Each correct group awards 100 points, while each incorrect guess costs 10 points and reduces your lives.

Where the Connections Come From

Connections is a word puzzle developed and published by The New York Times as part of The New York Times Games. It was released on June 12, 2023, during its beta phase. It is the second-most-played game published by the Times, behind Wordle. The game dynamically adjusts the difficulty based on your score, making it suitable for players of all skill levels.
